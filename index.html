Analyst by training, operator by experience.

I'm transitioning into cybersecurity from a frontline security operations role, where I spend my days monitoring systems, investigating incidents, and managing access controls. That work taught me the analyst fundamentals — triage under pressure, careful documentation, and escalating what matters — before I ever touched a SIEM.

Now I pair that operational instinct with formal training: a B.S. in Cybersecurity, an M.S. in Cyber Operations in progress, and the CompTIA Security+ certification. My focus areas are SOC monitoring and threat detection, incident response and forensics, and governance, risk, and compliance.

As a U.S. citizen eligible for a security clearance, I'm especially interested in roles defending critical systems — in the private sector or federal space.
